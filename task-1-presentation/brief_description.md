This Digital Literacy Awareness infographic serves as a comprehensive visual roadmap for navigating the modern digital landscape, moving systematically from 
foundational knowledge to professional application. It begins by defining digital literacy as the essential ability to find, evaluate, and communicate 
information across various devices, then transitions into practical resources by highlighting key collaboration and creative tools like Google Workspace and Canva.

Beyond mere productivity, the guide emphasizes the critical importance of cybersecurity through safe internet practices—such as implementing two-factor 
authentication and recognizing phishing scams—to protect one's digital identity. Finally, it explores how these skills translate into a professional online presence, 
focusing on the critical evaluation of tools to enhance workplace productivity. By blending a vibrant, four-stage design with actionable advice, the infographic 
provides a clear, structured framework for building the digital competence necessary for both academic success and long-term career growth in an increasingly 
tech-driven world.
